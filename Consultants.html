Built for defensibility — not just data entry

The whole exposure story, in one record

Field observations, sample plans, lab results, and calibration data converge on a single platform — the surrounding conditions and human factors behind every number, not just the number. That context is what separates a data point from a finding.

Guided collection. Standardized output.

Prompted questions — configured once by a CIH or project manager — enforce consistency and completeness on every survey. Analysis and reporting run on clean, comparable data instead of guesswork and follow-up calls.

Real-time visibility, zero windshield time

Review field entries as they happen, from anywhere. Catch a mis-set sampling train or a skipped calibration check while the pump is still running — not three weeks later at the desk.

Automation where the hours hide

Report generation, data transcription, and field-to-office handoffs run themselves. The administrative burden drops, and the hours return to strategic work — exposure assessment, controls, client counsel.

Defensible by design

Standardized collection methods, complete data sets, and detailed audit trails strengthen the defensibility of your findings — in litigation, exposure claims, and expert-witness work. When a report is challenged, the record answers for itself.
